About Me

My name is Paris Jerome also known as @theedemigawd on social media, I operate and reside in New York City. I am a digital artist, writer, illustrator and all around creator with experience and knowledge extending to Procreate, Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Premiere Pro.

My artwork was picked to be in the youth representation exhibition of Manhattan Borough Arts Festival in the Met for the Spring and Summer in 2018 and I have experiences working in creative direction, assistance and internship within a fast paced environment.

I have worked for the artists DUCKWRTH and Alex Mali under “The Blind Youth LLC” and have a journalism background from the ArtsConnection Corporation under their Reviewers and Critics Program as well. I am currently working on my own comic while studying visual art and have a desire to work in the creative story border and character developing field for games and TV shows.

My goal is to be a part of developing visually pleasing and dynamic characters allowing for more representation in media, more experimental stories and to open a door for being directly hands-on with developing my own medium of storytelling one day whether it be comics or animation. I am fully capable of getting things done effectively and efficiently while also being open to learning new things.
